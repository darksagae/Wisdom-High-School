About Us

About Wisdom High School - Rwimi

Wisdom High School - Rwimi is a privately owned secondary school committed to providing quality education in a conducive learning environment. Located in Central Ward, Rwimi Town Council, Bunyangabu District, we serve students from all religious denominations, offering both day and boarding facilities. Our school is fully licensed and registered by the Ministry of Education, Science, Technology and Sports.

We welcome students from all backgrounds and religious denominations who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth. We look at every applicant's academic performance and personal qualities to select students who will thrive in our God-fearing, holistic learning environment.

Our Philosophy

We believe in nurturing the whole person - mind, body, and spirit. Education is not just about academic achievement, but about developing character, values, and a sense of responsibility to serve God and humanity. We create an environment where students can discover their potential and grow into well-rounded individuals.

Our Principles

We are guided by integrity, excellence, respect, and service. We maintain high academic standards while fostering moral values and ethical behavior. Our commitment to quality education, combined with our God-fearing environment, ensures that students receive both intellectual and spiritual development.

Key Of Success

Success at Wisdom High School comes through dedication, hard work, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. We emphasize regular attendance, active participation, respect for teachers and peers, and a positive attitude toward learning. Our supportive environment and qualified teachers help students achieve their academic goals and personal growth.

Campus Tour

Visit our campus and see our modern facilities including science laboratories, computer lab, library, classrooms, and boarding facilities. Meet with our teachers and students to experience life at Wisdom High School. Schedule a tour to learn more about our O-Level and A-Level programs and see if Wisdom High School is the right choice for you.

Academic Excellence

Our students consistently achieve excellent results in UNEB examinations. We celebrate their achievements and prepare them for further education and future careers. Our comprehensive curriculum, qualified teachers, and supportive environment help students reach their full potential and succeed in their academic pursuits.

Student Achievements

Our students excel in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. Many of our graduates have gone on to pursue higher education at universities and colleges, while others have entered various professions. We take pride in the success of our students and their contributions to society.
