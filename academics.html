Academics

Our Academic Programs

Wisdom High School - Rwimi offers comprehensive academic programs for both O-Level and A-Level students. Our curriculum combines rigorous academic study with practical, hands-on experience to ensure our students are well-prepared for further education and future careers.

O-Level Program

Our four-year Ordinary Level program covers all core subjects including Mathematics, English, Sciences, and electives. Students study subjects like Kiswahili, CRE, Literature, History, Music, Art, and Computer Studies. This program prepares students for A-Level and future careers.

Learn More

A-Level Program

Our two-year Advanced Level program offers various subject combinations including MEG, BCM, BEM, PEM, PCM, HEA, LEA, and more. Students choose combinations that align with their career goals and university aspirations. This program prepares students for higher education.

Learn More

Holiday Learning Support

During school holidays, we offer online learning support and revision programs to help students stay engaged with their studies. These programs are designed to reinforce learning and prepare students for upcoming terms.

Learn More

Science Projects & Competitions

Students participate in science projects, science fairs, and academic competitions. These activities enhance practical skills, critical thinking, and provide opportunities to apply classroom knowledge in real-world contexts.

Learn More

Academic Departments

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics departments providing comprehensive science education for both O-Level and A-Level students.

Languages

English, Kiswahili, and Literature departments focusing on communication skills, reading comprehension, and language proficiency.

Social Studies

History, Geography, and Economics departments covering local and global perspectives, preparing students for social sciences at A-Level.

Religious Education

Christian Religious Education (CRE) department providing moral and spiritual guidance in a God-fearing environment.

Computer Studies

Computer Studies and ICT department teaching computer literacy, programming basics, and information technology skills.

Arts & Music

Art and Music departments fostering creativity, artistic expression, and cultural appreciation among students.

Education

Programs preparing the next generation of educators and educational leaders.
