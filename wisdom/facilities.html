Our Facilities

Modern Facilities for Excellence

Wisdom High School - Rwimi is equipped with modern facilities designed to support effective teaching and learning. Our infrastructure provides students with the resources they need to excel academically and develop holistically.

Learning Facilities

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Our science labs are designed to facilitate hands-on learning and practical experiments, helping students understand scientific concepts through direct experience.

  • Physics Laboratory
  • Chemistry Laboratory
  • Biology Laboratory

Computer Laboratory

Modern computer laboratory with up-to-date hardware and software. Students learn computer studies and ICT skills in a technology-rich environment that prepares them for the digital age.

  • Modern computers
  • Internet connectivity
  • Educational software

Library

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of textbooks, reference materials, novels, and educational resources. Our library serves both O-Level and A-Level students, providing quiet study spaces and research materials.

  • Extensive book collection
  • Reference materials
  • Quiet study areas

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids including whiteboards, projectors, and comfortable seating. Our classrooms provide an optimal learning environment for students.

  • Well-ventilated spaces
  • Modern teaching aids
  • Comfortable seating

Boarding Facilities

Our boarding facilities provide a safe, comfortable, and structured environment for students to live and learn.

Dormitories

Comfortable, well-maintained dormitories with adequate space for students. Separate facilities for boys and girls with proper supervision and security.

Dining Hall

Spacious dining hall serving nutritious, balanced meals. Our kitchen staff prepares healthy meals that meet dietary requirements and preferences.

Study Hall

Dedicated study areas for evening prep time. Supervised study sessions help students develop good study habits and complete assignments.

Recreation Areas

Recreational facilities including sports fields, common areas, and spaces for relaxation and social interaction among boarding students.

Additional Facilities

Sports Facilities

Sports fields and facilities for football, basketball, volleyball, and other athletic activities promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Security

24/7 security personnel and systems ensuring the safety and security of all students, staff, and school property.

Health Services

Sick bay and access to medical services for students' health and wellness needs during school hours.

Water & Sanitation

Clean water supply and modern sanitation facilities ensuring hygiene and comfort for all students.
