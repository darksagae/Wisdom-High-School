# Wisdom High School - Rwimi Website

A comprehensive, professional website for Wisdom High School - Rwimi, a licensed private secondary school in Bunyangabu District, Uganda.

## School Information

- **Name:** Wisdom High School - Rwimi
- **Location:** Central Ward, Rwimi Town Council, Bunyangabu District
- **Distance:** Approximately 40km along Fort Portal - Kasese Highway
- **License Number:** ME/32/5564
- **Registration Number:** PSS/W/55
- **UNEB Centre Number:** U3473
- **Registered By:** Ministry of Education, Science, Technology and Sports

## Vision & Mission

**Vision:** To develop into a centre of excellence in the region and beyond.

**Mission:** To provide a holistic education in a God-fearing environment for the service of God and humanity.

## Website Features

### Pages Created
1. **Home** - Hero section, quick stats, why choose us, vision & mission, programs overview
2. **About** - School history, vision, mission, core values, location information
3. **Academics** - O-Level and A-Level programs, learning facilities, academic calendar
4. **Admissions** - Requirements, application process, student options (day/boarding), important dates
5. **Facilities** - Science labs, computer lab, library, classrooms, boarding facilities
6. **News & Events** - Latest news, upcoming events, school updates
7. **Gallery** - Photo gallery with filtering (facilities, students, events, sports)
8. **Contact** - Contact information, contact form, map location, directions
