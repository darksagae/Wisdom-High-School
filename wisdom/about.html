About Wisdom High School

Welcome to Wisdom High School - Rwimi

Wisdom High School - Rwimi is a privately owned secondary school committed to providing quality education in a conducive learning environment. Located in Central Ward, Rwimi Town Council, Bunyangabu District, we serve students from all religious denominations, offering both day and boarding facilities.

Our school is fully licensed and registered by the Ministry of Education, Science, Technology and Sports, ensuring that we meet and exceed all educational standards. We provide comprehensive education for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) students.

Our Vision

To develop into a centre of excellence in the region and beyond, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and service to God and humanity. We strive to be a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals ready to make positive contributions to society.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education in a God-fearing environment for the service of God and humanity. We are committed to nurturing students' intellectual, spiritual, physical, and social development, preparing them to be responsible citizens and future leaders who serve their communities with integrity and compassion.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education, from academic performance to character development.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and moral principles in all our actions.

Service

We are committed to serving God and humanity through education and community engagement.

Inclusivity

We welcome students from all religious denominations, creating a diverse and respectful community.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and technology to enhance the learning experience.

Growth

We foster continuous growth and development in our students, staff, and institution.

Our Location

Wisdom High School - Rwimi is strategically located in Central Ward, Rwimi Town Council, Bunyangabu District. Our school is easily accessible, situated approximately 40 kilometers along the Fort Portal - Kasese highway.

The location provides a peaceful and conducive learning environment, away from the distractions of urban centers while remaining easily accessible to students and parents from surrounding areas.
