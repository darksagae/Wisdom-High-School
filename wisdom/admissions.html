Admissions

Join Wisdom High School - Rwimi

We welcome students from all backgrounds and religious denominations who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our God-fearing, holistic learning environment.

Admission Requirements

O-Level Entry (S1)

Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) certificate with good grades. Students should demonstrate academic potential and good character.

A-Level Entry (S5)

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 8 passes including English and Mathematics. Good performance in relevant subjects for chosen combination.

Required Documents

Birth certificate, academic transcripts, recommendation letters, medical report, and passport photos. All documents must be certified.

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the application form and submit all required documents to the school administration office or via email.

2

Review & Interview

Our admissions committee reviews applications and may conduct interviews with prospective students and parents.

3

Admission Decision

Successful applicants receive admission letters with details about enrollment, fees, and reporting dates.

4

Enrollment

Complete fee payment and registration to secure your place. Attend orientation and begin your journey at Wisdom High School.

Student Options

Day Students

Day students attend classes during school hours and return home in the evenings. This option is ideal for students living within reasonable distance from the school.

  • Regular school hours attendance
  • Access to all facilities
  • Participation in all activities

Boarding Students

Boarding students live on campus in comfortable, secure dormitories. This option provides a structured environment for focused learning and personal development.

  • Comfortable accommodation
  • 24/7 supervision and care
  • Structured study time
  • Meals and laundry services
