Why Choose Wisdom High School?

A Center of Excellence in Education

Licensed & Registered

Fully licensed by the Ministry of Education, Science, Technology and Sports. Registration number PSS/W/55, UNEB Centre U3473.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art science and computer laboratories, well-equipped library, and modern classrooms for optimal learning.

Day & Boarding

Comfortable boarding facilities and day student options. Safe, secure, and conducive learning environment for all students.

O-Level & A-Level

Comprehensive programs for both Ordinary Level and Advanced Level students with qualified, experienced teachers.

All Denominations

We welcome students from all religious denominations in an inclusive, respectful, and God-fearing environment.

Holistic Education

We provide holistic education that develops the mind, body, and spirit for the service of God and humanity.

Our Vision

To develop into a centre of excellence in the region and beyond, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and service to God and humanity.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education in a God-fearing environment for the service of God and humanity, preparing students to be responsible citizens and future leaders.
