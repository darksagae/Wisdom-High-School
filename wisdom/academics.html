Academics

Our Academic Programs

Wisdom High School - Rwimi offers comprehensive academic programs for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) students. Our curriculum is designed to provide a solid foundation for further education and career development, while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development.

Ordinary Level (O-Level) Program

Four-Year Comprehensive Secondary Education

Our O-Level program spans four years (S1 to S4) and provides students with a broad-based education covering all core subjects and electives. The program prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations administered by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB).

Core Subjects:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • History
  • Geography
  • Christian Religious Education / Islamic Religious Education

Advanced Level (A-Level) Program

Two-Year Specialized Advanced Education

Our A-Level program spans two years (S5 and S6) and offers specialized subject combinations to prepare students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. Students choose subject combinations based on their career interests and university aspirations.

Available Combinations:

  • Science (PCM, PCB, PEM)
  • Arts (HED, HEG, HGL)
  • Commerce (BAM, BAG, BCM)
  • General Paper (Compulsory)

Learning Facilities

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology practical work, enabling hands-on learning and experimentation.

Computer Laboratory

Modern computer laboratory with up-to-date technology and software for computer studies and ICT education.

Library

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of textbooks, reference materials, and reading resources for both O-Level and A-Level students.

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids and conducive learning environment.
