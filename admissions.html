Join Wisdom High School - Rwimi

We welcome students from all backgrounds and religious denominations who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth. We look at every applicant's academic performance and personal qualities to select students who will thrive in our God-fearing, holistic learning environment.

O-Level Admissions

Begin your secondary education journey at Wisdom High School - Rwimi. Our O-Level program welcomes students who have completed primary education and are ready to excel academically in our four-year Ordinary Level program.

A-Level Admissions

Continue your education with our A-Level program. We welcome students who have completed O-Level and are ready to pursue Advanced Level studies in their chosen subject combinations.

Apply Now

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We provide financial assistance to needy students, homeless students, and talented, dedicated students who demonstrate academic excellence. Our scholarships help ensure that financial circumstances do not prevent deserving students from accessing quality education.

Application Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents including previous school reports, birth certificate, passport photos, and recommendation letters. Applications can be submitted in person or online.

Step 2: Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness. This helps us understand each student's potential and learning needs.

Step 3: Interview

Selected candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for an interview. This allows us to understand the student's goals and ensure they are a good fit for our school community.

Step 4: Admission & Registration

Successful candidates will receive admission letters. Complete fee payment and registration to secure your place. Attend orientation and begin your journey at Wisdom High School.
